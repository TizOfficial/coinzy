Coinzy Bot - Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

To provide our economy bot services, we collect and process certain information about our users:

  • Discord User ID and Username
  • Server IDs where the bot is used
  • Economy-related activity data
  • Transaction history and balances
  • Command usage patterns
  • Inventory and item ownership data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing your virtual economy account
  • Processing in-game transactions
  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Improving bot features and performance
  • Generating anonymous usage statistics
  • Providing customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure database encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited staff access to user data
  • Automated backup systems
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • For essential bot functionality

5. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to request data deletion
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Active accounts: As long as the account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Up to 12 months
  • Transaction history: 24 months
  • Deleted accounts: 30 days before permanent deletion

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our bot does not use cookies but may collect:

  • Command usage patterns
  • Feature interaction statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years of age. If we discover we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through our Discord server.
